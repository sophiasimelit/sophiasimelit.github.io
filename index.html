Sophia Simelitidou

I am a aspiring anthropologist with a bit of academic training in theatrical theory. I just finished my masters in Panteion University of Athens in Social and Cultural Anthropology and I currently live in Edinburgh.

I could try to write more about myself but I think I have a fear of over-exposition; that is why my main research interest is Digital Media and more specifically Social Media and the images of exposed lives one can see there. My research interests also include gender and Islam.
